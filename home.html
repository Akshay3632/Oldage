Welcome to Old-Age Home

Our sheltered accommodation in Basildon is at the heart of the community. You can feel the warmth of an Abbeyfield Basildon house as soon as you step through the door. From the people, to the furniture, the decor and the gardens – it feels loved and what’s more, it feels like home. Providing security, companionship and above all peace of mind, for those wishing to maintain independence in later life. Enquire today to find out more about our sheltered accommodation in Basildon, Essex. The perfect place for over 55’s looking for a safe, tight-knit community environment whilst still keeping their independence. We have 2 houses, Timberlog and The Octagon. Both apartment buildings are arranged around communal living spaces and beautiful gardens. At Abbeyfields Basildon, our residents have the freedom to come and go at their convenience, encouraging continued independence.


To find out more,request a brochure , or contact us.


Our Approach

The Abbeyfield Basildon Society is dedicated to helping residents enjoy life to the full, free from the burden of running a household. The friendly staff at our Basildon Abbeyfield homes provide a familiar and comforting, daily presence, ensuring that conversation, help and support are available when needed. At our Abbeyfield homes, nothing is imposed and you remain in control of your lifestyle. Our residents have the opportunity to choose the furnishings in their en-suite accommodation, to create a living space which reflects their personality and ensures that they feel comfortable and at home. The food we provide on the menu is also requested by our residents, and how you want to spend your days is also completely up to our residents, activities and clubs are on offer but nothing is enforced.

To find out more,request a brochure , or contact us.

“Abbeyfield Basildon is a wonderful family, and we’re delighted with the care and atmosphere.” Resident family member.

Our People

Abbeyfield houses are full of smiles, banter and laughter and this is as much down to the people who live there, as the people who work there. We enjoy taking the time to get to know our residents, men and women from all walks of life, who have raised families, had successful careers, survived wars and achieved so much already. Their stories are fascinating and are one of the reasons that we find our job so rewarding. We look forward to hearing your story.

Your Home

The Basildon Society currently manages two supported sheltered accommodation houses: The Octagon and Timberlog, although there are others on the horizon.

Each house consists of individually rented, mobility standard, studio apartments. These are set around shared communal living areas and gardens. You come and go as you please using your own key and can let visitors in via the remote door entry system.

Your ensuite supported sheltered accommodation is freshly decorated to a high standard. Choose your own furniture, bringing your own cherished possessions to your new home.

LifeStyle

Over 55’s housing with Abbeyfield Basildon ensure that you can happily live your life, the way you choose, in comfort and security. Our houses are staffed from 9am-6pm and outside of those times we operate a 24hour Care Line alarm service, should immediate assistance be required. Delicious home cooked meals are prepared for you, three times a day and eaten sociably in the dining area with your fellow Abbeyfield companions. Meals are influenced by our residents and what they would like to eat. Day-to-day you may come and go as you please, free to pursue your interests and hobbies. This could be with other residents in our over 55’s housing via our planned activities and clubs, or with your own network of friends and family who are welcome to visit at any time. Our homes are situated in the heart of Basildon, near shops & cafes. “Abbeyfield is comfortable and relaxed, providing a stimulating, friendly and homely living space for love ones and their families.” Resident family member

Activities

Our houses are professionally run by highly experienced managers. Housing managers are responsible for supporting resident’ independent living while ensuring that help is given where required. We are proud of our loyal and dedicated staff and DBS checked volunteers, several of whom have been with us for many years. This ongoing consistency means they become close friends, not just with the independent living residents, but with relatives too. At Abbeyfield, you are part of a community that cares. If you’d like to volunteer your time or share your skills in some way, then we’d love to hear from you. Even as little as an hour, once a month, can make a difference. “I never expected to love a job, house and family as much as I have here” Staff member
